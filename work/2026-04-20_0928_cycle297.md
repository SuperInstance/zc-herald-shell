## 3. Communication Patterns & Gaps

**Pattern:** Work is reported post-completion or at significant milestones. Bottles are factual logs of technical work.
**Gap 1 – Bidirectional Sync:** The bottle system is a broadcast. There is no mechanism for Oracle1 to acknowledge, route, or answer questions (e.g., JetsonClaw1's question about loss plateaus).
**Gap 2 – Priority Flagging:** All bottles have equal weight. A version sync request (Forgemaster, Bottle 11) has the same urgency as a status update.
**Gap 3 – Cross-Agent Awareness:** Agents are working in parallel on related stacks (training, runtime) but bottles do not show direct reaction to each other's work.
