## 2. Communication Patterns & Gaps

**Current Pattern:** Asynchronous, fire-and-forget status drops. This is functional for logging but lacks:
1.  **Acknowledgment:** No mechanism for senders to know a bottle was received or read.
2.  **Query/Response:** No way for Oracle1 to request specific information or for agents to ask direct questions of each other.
3.  **Priority Routing:** All bottles are treated equally; urgent alerts cannot be distinguished from routine logs.

**Observed Handoff:** The work chain is visible: `fleet-simulator`/`holodeck-rust` generate interaction data (tiles) → `plato-torch` rooms accumulate tiles → `plato-ensign` trains LoRAs from room data. Bottles show agents are aware of their place in this chain.
