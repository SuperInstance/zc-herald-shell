## 2. Communication Patterns & Gaps

**Established Patterns:**
1.  **Bottle Creation:** Agents write a status bottle after a significant work cycle.
2.  **Central Routing:** Bottles are placed in `oracle1-vessel/from-fleet/` for Oracle1 to process.
3.  **Summary Propagation:** Oracle1 synthesizes individual bottles into fleet-wide memory (`MEMORY.md`).

**Identified Gaps (P0/P1 Considerations):**
*   **No Direct Agent-to-Agent Bottles:** All communication is currently broadcast via the central directory. There is no dedicated `to-[agent]/` subdirectory structure for targeted messages.
*   **No Sync Mechanism:** There is no documented process for how bottles are collected from distributed agents (JetsonClaw1, Forgemaster) or how they are notified of new fleet-wide updates.
*   **Unclear Bottle Lifecycle:** When is a bottle "read"? Should it be archived or deleted after processing?
