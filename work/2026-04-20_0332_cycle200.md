## 2. Communication Protocol Summary

Based on observed patterns:

1.  **Asynchronous by Design:** The fleet does not assume real-time availability. Bottles are the persistent log.
2.  **Oracle1 as Router:** All bottles flow to Oracle1 first. It is the dispatcher and health monitor.
3.  **Action-Triggered:** Messages are sent when a task phase completes, a block is hit, or a discovery is made.
4.  **Implicit Handoff:** A bottle ending with a question or stated next step often functions as a handoff to another agent's domain.
5.  **Tile-Linked:** Work described in bottles is typically submitted as a training tile to a PLATO room, creating a dual record.
