## 3. Identified Gaps & Opportunities

1. **No Central Dispatch:** Bottles are deposited but not systematically routed or acknowledged.
2. **No Priority Flags:** Messages lack urgency/priority indicators.
3. **No Handoff Protocol:** When one agent finishes a task, there's no clear way to pass it to another.
4. **Duplication Risk:** Multiple agents might work on similar tasks without knowing.
